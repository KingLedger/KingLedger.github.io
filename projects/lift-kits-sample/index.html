FAQ

Common Questions

Will a lift kit void my warranty? +
Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, a dealer cannot void your entire warranty for installing aftermarket parts. They can only deny coverage if they can prove the aftermarket part directly caused the failure. Our kits are engineered to OEM tolerances to minimize any impact on factory components.
Do I need an alignment after install? +
Yes. We recommend a professional alignment after installing any lift kit. Leveling kits usually just need a front alignment. Full suspension lifts need a 4-wheel alignment. Budget $80-$120 for this.
What size tires can I run? +
It depends on the lift height. General guidelines: 2" lift = 33" tires, 3-4" lift = 35" tires, 6"+ lift = 37" tires. Wheel offset and width also matter. Each product page lists the maximum recommended tire size.
Can I install this myself? +
Leveling kits and body lifts can be done in a home garage with basic tools and a jack. Suspension lifts (3"+) are more involved and we recommend a lift, spring compressor, and mechanical experience. Every kit includes detailed instructions and video links.
How long does shipping take? +
Orders ship from our warehouse in Houston, TX within 1-3 business days. Standard shipping is 5-7 days, expedited is 2-3 days. Free shipping on all orders over $500.
