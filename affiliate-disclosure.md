## Affiliate Disclosure

**The Iron Ledger** believes in full transparency with our readers.

### Amazon Associates Disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases. This site contains affiliate links — if you buy through them, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

### How Affiliate Links Work

Some of the links on this site are affiliate links. This means that if you click on the link and purchase an item, The Iron Ledger will receive an affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. Regardless, we only recommend products or services that we believe will add value to our readers.

### Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running a blog takes time, effort, and money. Affiliate income helps us:

- Keep the site free for all readers
- Continue researching and reviewing products
- Produce high-quality, in-depth content
- Cover hosting and operational costs

### Our Promise

We will **never** recommend a product solely because it pays a commission. Every book, tool, and product featured on The Iron Ledger has been vetted and chosen because we genuinely believe it provides value.

If we don't think something is worth your money, we won't recommend it — period.
