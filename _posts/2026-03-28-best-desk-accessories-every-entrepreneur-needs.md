## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the single most impactful desk accessory?

A monitor arm or stand. Getting your screen at the right height prevents neck strain and opens up desk space. If you're spending 8+ hours at your desk, this is where your money should go first.

### Do I really need a desk mat?

If you care about your desk surface, yes. It also provides a consistent mouse tracking area and makes your setup look significantly more professional. At $13, it's the cheapest upgrade with the biggest visual impact.

### How do I manage cables behind a standing desk?

Use the JOTO cable sleeves for vertical cable runs and an under-desk tray for horizontal ones. The key is giving cables enough slack to accommodate the desk moving between sitting and standing positions. Check out our guide to the [best standing desks for entrepreneurs under $500](/best-standing-desks-entrepreneurs-under-500/) for more on this.

### Is a USB-C hub worth it over a full docking station?

For most entrepreneurs, yes. A hub is portable, affordable, and covers 90% of connectivity needs. You only need a full docking station if you're running multiple 4K displays at 60Hz or need Thunderbolt passthrough.

### What desk accessories pair well with a standing desk?

All of these work great with a standing desk. The monitor arm is especially important because it moves with you as you raise and lower the desk. Pair these accessories with one of the [best standing desks under $500](/best-standing-desks-entrepreneurs-under-500/) for the ultimate setup.
