## FAQ

### Are over-ear headphones better than earbuds for deep work?

Generally yes. Over-ear headphones provide better noise isolation, more comfortable long-term wear, and stronger ANC due to the physical seal around your ears. But if you find over-ears too warm or heavy, premium ANC earbuds like the Sony WF-1000XM5 are a legitimate alternative.

### How important is noise cancellation vs just playing music?

ANC makes a significant difference, especially in environments with consistent background noise (HVAC, traffic, office chatter). Music alone masks noise; ANC eliminates it. The combination of ANC + music creates a focus environment that's dramatically better than either alone.

### Can I use noise-cancelling headphones for video calls?

Yes, and most premium options now have excellent microphones for calls. The Sony XM5 and Bose QC Ultra both have beam-forming microphones that isolate your voice. However, dedicated headsets with boom microphones still outperform wireless headphones for call quality.

### Will noise-cancelling headphones damage my hearing?

ANC itself doesn't damage hearing — it reduces noise without adding volume. In fact, ANC can protect your hearing by allowing you to listen at lower volumes since you're not competing with background noise. Just keep your music at reasonable levels.

### How long should I wear headphones during a deep work session?

Take breaks every 60-90 minutes — not just for your ears, but for your focus. The Pomodoro technique (25 minutes on, 5 minutes off) or 90-minute deep work blocks both work well. Give your ears a break, move around, then lock back in.
