## FAQ

### Is Korean skincare actually better than Western skincare?

Not inherently "better" — but the K-beauty philosophy differs in important ways. Korean skincare emphasizes gentle, layered hydration and preventive care rather than aggressive treatment. Western skincare tends to go harder on actives (retinol, high-percentage acids) which work well but can also cause more irritation. The best routine probably combines elements of both. That said, K-beauty consistently offers better value — comparable ingredients at 50-70% lower price points.

### Are these products safe for sensitive skin?

Most products on this list are suitable for sensitive skin, with the exception of the medicube Zero Pore Pads (which contain AHA/BHA) and the Age-R device. The ANUA Heartleaf Toner and COSRX Snail Mucin are specifically beloved by sensitive skin users. Start with one new product at a time and patch test on your inner wrist or behind your ear.

### Do I need to do the full 10-step Korean skincare routine?

Absolutely not. The "10-step routine" is a marketing concept that even most Korean people don't follow daily. 3-4 steps (cleanser, toner/essence, moisturizer, sunscreen) is plenty. Add targeted treatments as needed. The products on this list can slot into any routine length.

### Why is snail mucin in skincare? Is it cruel?

Snail mucin in K-beauty is collected from Cryptomphalus aspersa snails that are placed on mesh nets in controlled environments. They naturally secrete mucin as they move. Reputable brands like COSRX don't harm the snails in collection. The mucin is then filtered and purified. If the concept still bothers you, the ANUA Heartleaf Toner and Beauty of Joseon Glow Serum offer similar hydrating benefits from plant-based ingredients.

### How long before I see results?

- **Hydration improvement:** Immediate to 1 week
- **Reduced redness:** 1-2 weeks
- **Pore refinement:** 2-4 weeks
- **Brightening/evening tone:** 3-6 weeks
- **Anti-aging benefits:** 6-12 weeks

Consistency matters more than intensity. Using products daily at lower strengths outperforms aggressive weekly treatments for most skin concerns.
